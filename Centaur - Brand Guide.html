01

Logo and mark

The wordmark is Newsreader Medium. The mark is a reading instrument: an ink ring with a single accent pip, a value resting on a dial. It states what the product is without illustration or cliche.

Centaur

Centaur

Reversed, on night

The mark, up close

The ring is a single ink stroke (the two halves of the path close into one continuous circle). It is the floor of the brand, drawn in nothing but ink.

The pip is the one piece of accent in the whole mark. It marks a reading on the dial, which is the only job the accent ever has.

Minimum clear space equals the wordmark cap height on all sides. Do not recolor the ring, fill the pip with anything but the accent, rotate the mark, or add effects.

02

Color

A two color floor carries the entire interface: cool paper and cool ink. One accent sits on top of that floor, and it is rationed. The accent appears only where something changes state: a reading, a focus ring, the primary action, an active toggle. It is never decoration.

No traffic lights. Direct, Delegate, and Defer are never coded green, amber, or red. They are told apart by form and weight (where the reading sits on the scale), so the distinction survives for anyone who cannot rely on color. Centaur reports a measurement; it does not flag a winner.

04

The categories

Every handoff is read as one of three named choices. They sit on a single axis: how much of the thinking the person keeps. The reading is a position and a weight, never a color. A Defer is a measurement, not a moral failure; deferring is sometimes the right call.

You hold the thinkingAI holds the thinking
DirectDelegateDefer

Direct

You hold the thinking. The AI is a sounding board; the judgment stays with you.

Delegate

You hand over a bounded outcome and inspect the result. The thinking is shared; you still check the work.

Defer

You outsource the judgment itself. The AI decides; you accept.

05

Drift indicator

On the History screen, one card outranks everything else: a single number and one plain sentence that states what the number is. It is a measurement, not a guilt trip. The user can turn it off at any time and the product keeps classifying and logging.

07

Voice

Sharp, dry, specific. Centaur talks like a competent colleague telling you the truth, not a coach welcoming you to an app. It never flatters and never scolds. No motivational language, no exclamation points, no em dashes or en dashes.

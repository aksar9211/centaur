The Centaur thesis
01 · The idea

Three ways to hand work to AI.

A centaur is one creature, half human and half horse: the oldest picture of a person and a powerful engine moving as one, with the person still steering. Whether AI extends you or replaces you comes down to how you hand off the work. There are three ways to do it.

Direct

You hold the thinking. The AI is a sounding board; the judgment stays with you.

Delegate

You hand over a bounded outcome and inspect the result. The thinking is shared; you still check the work.

Defer

You outsource the judgment itself. The AI decides; you accept. Sometimes that is the right call, and Centaur says so.

03 · Built to stay honest

An instrument you can trust to report, not to manipulate.

Three controls ship in the first build. They are not polish. They keep the product from becoming the thing it set out to fix.

Every verdict shows its evidence

There is no black box reading. The reasoning that produced a classification is always on screen, in plain language.

You can override any reading

Mark any classification as wrong, on every verdict. The override is logged, so your record stays honest.

The drift indicator is optional

Turn it off whenever you want. Centaur keeps classifying and logging; it just stops surfacing the running ratio.
